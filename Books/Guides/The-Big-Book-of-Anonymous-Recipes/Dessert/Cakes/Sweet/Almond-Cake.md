
## Almond Cake ##

### Ingredients ###


* 250g ground almonds
* 200g melted butter
* 4 eggs
* 1 pinch of salt

* warning: prepare 2 days in advance

### The making ###

Mix everything in a well buttered tin, cook 45mins on low setting

1. serve with a sabayon icing and almonds

### Sabayon ###

#### Ingredients ####

* 8 egg yolks
* 1 bottle of french muscat 75cl

#### The making ####

1. beat the egg yolks in the wine without adding any sugar
2. cook in a bain-marie while whisking it continuously until it thickens
can be consumed warm or cold
