### Peanut Butter Blossom Cookies ###

#### Ingredients ####

[[!img Pbblossom.jpg size="200x200"]]

* Semi-sweet chocolate kisses.
* 1/2 cup butter
* 3/4 cup peanut butter (creamy preferred)
* 1/3 cup granulated sugar
* 1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
* 1 egg
* 2 tablespoons milk
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 to 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 (or less) cup of Granulated sugar, set aside in a dish or bowl for later use.

#### Directions ####

1. Heat oven to 375Â°F. Remove chocolates from wrappers (if applicable)
2. Beat butter and peanut butter in large bowl until well-blended. Add sugar and brown sugar; beat until fluffy. Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually beat into peanut butter mixture.
3. Roll dough into 1-inch spheres. Cover in granulated sugar, conveniently set aside; place on ungreased cookie sheet.
4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes (or until lightly browned). Immediately press a chocolate into center of each cookie; cookie will crack.
5. Let cookies cool on a rack or on a plate. Avoid stacking, and avoid touching the chocolate until cooled.
