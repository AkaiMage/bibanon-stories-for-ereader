## Carrot Cake ##

### Ingredients ###

* 4 eggs
* 250g of flour
* grated lemon skin
* 250g of ground almonds
* 200g of carrots
* 1 large tablespoon of (potato) flour
* 1/2 a tablespoon of baking powder

### Making ###

* finely grate the carrots
* separate egg yolks from white
* mix yolks with sugar until you obtain a foamy paste
* add the grated lemon skin, almonds and carrots
* mix, add flour and baking powder
* mix again
* beat the egg whites until firm
* incorporate them mixing from bottom to top of the dough
* put in a buttered and floured tin
* bake 45mins on medium heat
* may be iced with zested sugar
