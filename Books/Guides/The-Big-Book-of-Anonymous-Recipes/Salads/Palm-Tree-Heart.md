## Ingredients ##

* 500g og palm tree heart
* 2 limes
* 3tablespoons of olive oil
* salt, pepper

## Making ##

1. remove the fibrous outside of the heart if there is one
2. finely cut the heart
  * at the same time press the limes over it
  * use a stainless knife to prevent oxydation of the heart
3. put oil on it, add salt and pepper, serve as fresh as possible
