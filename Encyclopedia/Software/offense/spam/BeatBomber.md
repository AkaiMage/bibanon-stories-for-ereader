**BeatBomber** is a neato spam program that runs directly from your machine. No SMTP server necessary. Just make a Gmail account behind a proxy, plug this into [Prox/i/fier](/Prox/i/fier) and test it out on yourself with a few messages. Allow ~5 minutes. If it works then spam your enemy.

## Usage ##


1. Download the program. 
1. Open the program. 
1. Input your victim's email address. 
1. Input your Gmail username and password. 
1. Press Bombs Away and it will barrage the victim. 
1. Press Done Bombing
