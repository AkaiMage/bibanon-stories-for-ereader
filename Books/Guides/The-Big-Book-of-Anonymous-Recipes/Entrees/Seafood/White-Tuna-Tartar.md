## Ingredients ##

* 1kg of raw tuna
* 4 shallots
* 4 lemons
* parsley
* chervil
* chive
* aioli
* 4 tomatoes

## Making it ##

1. chop the tuna in a CHOPPPER
2. soak with lemon juice
3. salt, pepper
4. mix with shallots and herbs
5. let rest at least 2 hours
6. serve with aioli and peeled and cut tomatoes
