## Olive oil potato mash ##

### Ingredients ###

* 1kg of potatoes
* 25cl of milk
* 2tablespoons of cream
* 1 glass of very fine quality olive oil

### The making ###

1. crush the cooked potatoes in either a mash making tool, or by hand
  * cook them a bit longer if you want this step to be easier
2. heat up in a pot by slowly adding the oil and stirring
3. add boiling milk and cream
4. Serve on the spot
  * you may add basil and/or ground olive
  * good for veal, poultry or roasted fishes
