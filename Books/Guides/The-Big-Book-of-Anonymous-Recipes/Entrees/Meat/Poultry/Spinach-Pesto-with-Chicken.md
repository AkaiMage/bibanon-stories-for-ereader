## Ingredients ##

* 1 lb boneless chicken breasts
* pasta of your choice 
* 2 large handfuls of fresh spinach leaves (stems removed)
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* 2-3 cloves garlic
* 1/3 cup pine nuts
* grated parmesan
* paprika
* salt and pepper
* 2-3 sun dried tomatoes (optional)

## Preparation ##

* slice the chicken breasts into half-inch-wide strips and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
* pan fry chicken in non-stick skillet or with a small amount of olive oil until cooked through.
* cook pasta according to package
* while pasta and chicken are cooking combine olive oil, spinach, garlic, cheese, paprika, nuts, salt and pepper in a blender or food processor. Blend until soupy, adding more spinach or oil as needed for desired consistency.
* toss cooked pasta and chicken with sauce
* slice tomatoes into thin strips and use as garnish
