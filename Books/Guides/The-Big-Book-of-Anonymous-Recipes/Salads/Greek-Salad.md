## Ingredients ##

* 4-6 tomatoes
* 1 cucumber
* 8-10 black olives
* 1 red onion (optional)
* 100-200 g Feta cheese

Salad dressing:

* 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil
* 1/2-1 tablespoon vinegar(or lemon juice) 

## Making ##

Wash the tomatoes, cut everything to bits, mix the dressing, throw it all together. Having the cheese on top might look better. Enjoy!
