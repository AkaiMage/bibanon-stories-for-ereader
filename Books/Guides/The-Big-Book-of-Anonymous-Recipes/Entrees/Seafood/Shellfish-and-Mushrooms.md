## Ingredients: ##

* 200g of shellfish flesh
* 400g of mushrooms (the kind you find in woods, whatever)
* 10cl of liquid cream
* 20g of butter
* salt, pepper

## Preparation: ##

* remove the roots of the mushrooms and quickly wash them in water
* heat the butter in a pan, add the mushrooms and let cook on low heat for 10 mins
* add the shellfish and cream
* salt and pepper
* let it cook for an additional 5 minutes
  * take small bowl and heat them up if possible
* share the result into said bowls
